Welcome! I'm a hobby-photographer located near Augsburg, Germany.

I am currently working mostly in black & white with urban, streetphotography, architecture and landscape. I'm interested in everyday scenes and people, trying to find new perspectives on our daily life.

My photography is not my regular job and only non-commercial, so I just do it for exploring the visual space.

Ocassionally, I also do model / portrait shootings - non-commercially and on a time-for-print basis (TFP). Contact me if you are interested.

If you like my work have a look at my Instagram.

Peter
